About

Machines that see.
Systems that scale.

I'm a first-year MEng Robotics student at University of Maryland, College Park, specializing in perception robotics and computer vision. Before grad school, I spent 2+ years as an ML Engineer at Clutterbot, where I built and shipped production grade perception stacks solving problems like obstacle avoidance, metric depth estimation, object detection, sensur fusion and 3D Reconstruction.

My work sits at the intersection of 3D scene understanding, deep learning, and real-world robotics. I care about systems that actually work outside the lab: robust, efficient, deployable.

Currently exploring multimodal vision (audio,tactile, X modalities with vision), planning algorithms, and control theory through coursework and research projects.
